Gathering for a wedding, retreat, or family getaway is easier when everyone can stay together.

At The Show Barn, our on-site cabins and lodges provide comfort, convenience, and unforgettable Bluegrass views just steps away from the venue itself.

For couples and planners, this means less stress and more time together — no juggling hotel blocks, no coordinating scattered travel. Everyone you love can wake up, celebrate, and wind down in one place.

Looking for something even more seamless? You can customize your stay

by reserving just the cabins you need or securing all of our properties for exclusive use. For full-property reservations or tailored lodging options, contact us directly to create the arrangement that's right for your group.
